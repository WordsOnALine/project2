What is Cirno Fumo?

Cirno Fumo is a plush of a character called Cirno from the popular Japanese series Touhou. Fumos themselves are soft, high quality plushies. Cirno is a fairy who wields mastery over the powers of ice and is quite childish and idiotic at times. However, because I do not want to incorperate any of those in this test demo, it is just Cirno Fumo in appearance.
